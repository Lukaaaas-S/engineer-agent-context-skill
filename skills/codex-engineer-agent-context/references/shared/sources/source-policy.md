# Source Policy

## Source Use

- Keep per-skill provenance in `skills/<skill>/references/source-map.md`.
- Read source maps only when provenance, boundary checks, or deeper pattern detail is needed.
- Use the minimum source material needed for the task.

## Boundary Use

- Product skills may consume ICP, positioning, pricing, eval, and GTM facts, but they do not own GTM execution.
- GTM skills may consume product facts, but they do not write PRDs or product specs.
- AEO skills may consume approved positioning and product facts, but they do not invent strategy or product claims.
- Agent skills may consume product, GTM, or AEO requirements, but they do not redefine those domains.
